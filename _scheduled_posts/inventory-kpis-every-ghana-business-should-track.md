You cannot improve what you do not measure. Inventory KPIs turn stock data into decisions.

## 10 essential inventory KPIs

1. Stock turnover ratio.
2. Gross margin return on inventory (GMROI).
3. Stockout rate.
4. Fill rate.
5. Dead stock percentage.
6. Inventory accuracy percentage.
7. Shrinkage rate.
8. Expiry loss rate (for sensitive products).
9. Days inventory outstanding (DIO).
10. Reorder cycle adherence.

## Weekly dashboard for owners

At minimum, review:

- Top stockouts this week.
- Top dead stock lines.
- Stock value trend.
- Variance report by branch/category.

## KPI implementation tip

Start with 4 KPIs first (stockout rate, accuracy %, dead stock %, shrinkage rate), then expand after team adoption.

## Final takeaway

Inventory KPIs help Ghana businesses move from reactive firefighting to proactive control. A simple dashboard reviewed consistently can transform performance.
