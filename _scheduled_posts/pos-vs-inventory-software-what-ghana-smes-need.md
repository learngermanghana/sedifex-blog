Many business owners ask: should I start with a POS system or inventory software?

## Core difference

- **POS software** focuses on checkout, receipts, and daily sales.
- **Inventory software** focuses on stock levels, purchasing, transfers, and stock value.

A modern business usually needs both in one connected platform.

## What happens if you use POS only

You may sell fast, but later struggle with:

- Unknown stock value.
- Frequent stockouts.
- Weak purchase planning.
- High shrinkage and losses.

## What happens if you use inventory only

You may track stock, but daily selling becomes slow and fragmented without proper cashier workflows.

## Recommended setup for Ghana SMEs

1. Start with integrated POS + inventory.
2. Add customer tracking and simple CRM next.
3. Introduce branch-level controls as you grow.

## Decision rule

If you do more than 20 transactions daily and manage more than 100 SKUs, integrated software is usually the right choice.

## Final takeaway

For most Ghana SMEs, POS vs inventory software is not either-or. The winning setup is one connected system that supports both sales speed and stock accuracy.
