As retailers open more branches, stock visibility becomes harder and losses can increase.

## Common multi-branch pain points

- One branch overstocked while another runs out.
- Transfers recorded late or not at all.
- Different pricing and process standards.
- Head office lacks real-time branch view.

## Strong operating model

1. Use one centralized product catalog.
2. Standardize receiving and transfer workflows.
3. Require transfer approval and confirmation.
4. Monitor branch-level stock variance weekly.
5. Set branch-specific min/max stock thresholds.

## Reports every owner should review

- Branch stock value.
- Fast movers by location.
- Inter-branch transfer turnaround time.
- Shrinkage/adjustment trends by branch.

## Final takeaway

Multi-branch control is a systems and process challenge. Central visibility plus disciplined branch routines is the fastest path to stable growth.
