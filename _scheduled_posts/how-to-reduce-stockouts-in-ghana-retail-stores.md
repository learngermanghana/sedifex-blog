Stockouts hurt revenue twice: you lose immediate sales and may lose future customer trust.

## Why stockouts happen in Ghana retail

Typical causes include:

- No clear reorder levels per product.
- Inaccurate manual stock counts.
- Delayed supplier restocking.
- Fast-moving SKUs not reviewed daily.
- Branch transfers not tracked in real time.

## 7-step stockout reduction process

1. Classify your top 20% revenue products.
2. Set minimum stock levels per SKU.
3. Set reorder points using average weekly sales.
4. Track supplier lead time in days.
5. Enable low-stock alerts.
6. Review stockout report weekly.
7. Create backup suppliers for critical items.

## Simple reorder formula

**Reorder Point = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time in Days) + Safety Stock**

This formula helps avoid emergencies and panic buying.

## Team discipline matters

Even good software fails if staff skip receiving, sales posting, or transfer entries. Assign ownership per shift and audit compliance weekly.

## Final takeaway

Reducing stockouts is not guesswork. With reorder rules, clear responsibility, and consistent reporting, Ghana retail stores can keep shelves full and customers loyal.
