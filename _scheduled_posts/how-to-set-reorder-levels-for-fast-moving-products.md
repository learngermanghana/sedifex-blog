Fast-moving products need tighter reorder control than slow sellers.

## Step 1: Get reliable sales velocity

Measure average daily sales for each SKU over the last 8 to 12 weeks.

## Step 2: Confirm supplier lead time

Lead time is the number of days between placing and receiving an order.

## Step 3: Add safety stock

Safety stock protects you from demand spikes and supplier delays.

## Formula

**Reorder Level = (Average Daily Sales × Lead Time) + Safety Stock**

## Practical example

If a product sells 15 units/day, lead time is 6 days, and safety stock is 30 units:

Reorder level = (15 × 6) + 30 = **120 units**

## Review cadence

- Review top fast-movers weekly.
- Review mid-tier products bi-weekly.
- Adjust for seasonality and promotions.

## Final takeaway

Reorder levels are not static. Use data, revise regularly, and focus on fast movers first to reduce lost sales.
