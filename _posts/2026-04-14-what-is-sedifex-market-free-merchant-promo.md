If you are asking, **"What is www.sedifexmarket.com?"**

Sedifex Market is a platform built to help stores get **free merchant promotion**.

Stores do not submit directly to Sedifex Market. Instead, they save their products or services on [Sedifex.com](https://www.sedifex.com), and their data is synced automatically.

For a store to appear on Sedifex Market, the store must have a Sedifex account and pass team verification. Verified stores receive extra public exposure so more people can discover the business.

![Small business owner using a laptop in a modern retail store](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556742049-0cfed4f6a45d?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1600&q=80)

## How It Works

1. **Store creates a Sedifex account** and saves products or services on Sedifex.com.
2. **Product/service data syncs automatically** to Sedifex Market.
3. **Sedifex verifies the store** for marketplace visibility.
4. **Verified stores receive free exposure** through our merchant visibility channels.

Stores that are not yet verified can still use all Sedifex features, but they will not appear on Sedifex Market until verification is complete.

## Why This Matters for Small Businesses

Many small businesses have good products and good events, but low visibility.

Sedifex Market helps build that bridge:

- Better visibility for local stores
- More trust through store verification
- Smarter promotion without extra ad costs

In short, we help small businesses **work smart, stay visible, and grow**.
