If you are asking, **"What is www.sedifexmarket.com?"**

Sedifex Market is a platform built to help stores get **free merchant promotion**.

When a store saves an event or promo activity, our team reviews and verifies it. After verification, we give that store extra public exposure so more people can discover the business.

![Small business owner using a laptop in a modern retail store](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1556742049-0cfed4f6a45d?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1600&q=80)

## How It Works

1. **Store submits an event** on Sedifex Market.
2. **Sedifex verifies the event** to keep promotions trustworthy.
3. **Verified stores receive free exposure** through our merchant visibility channels.

This process helps businesses promote real offers with confidence.

## Why This Matters for Small Businesses

Many small businesses have good products and good events, but low visibility.

Sedifex Market helps build that bridge:

- Better visibility for local stores
- More trust through event verification
- Smarter promotion without extra ad costs

In short, we help small businesses **work smart, stay visible, and grow**.
